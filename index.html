China's Iconic Skyscrapers

Explore some of the tallest and most stunning buildings in China

Shanghai Tower

Height: 632 meters

Location: Shanghai

The tallest building in China and the second tallest in the world, known for its twisting design and sustainable technology.

Ping An Finance Center

Height: 599 meters

Location: Shenzhen

This skyscraper is the 4th tallest in the world, featuring office space, hotels, and retail centers.

Guangzhou CTF Finance Centre

Height: 530 meters

Location: Guangzhou

A mixed-use skyscraper that includes apartments, hotels, and offices, known for its sleek and elegant structure.

CITIC Tower (China Zun)

Height: 528 meters

Location: Beijing

Shaped like an ancient wine vessel called “Zun,” this is Beijing’s tallest building and a symbol of the city’s skyline.
